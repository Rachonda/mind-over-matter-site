About Me

Hi, I’m Monica Douglas — a Board Certified and Licensed Clinical Professional Counselor in Maryland with a deep passion for helping others navigate life’s challenges and rediscover their inner strength. Since 2020, I’ve been proudly providing mental health services where I support individuals on their journey toward healing, growth, and self-discovery. I hold a Master of Arts in Clinical Mental Health Counseling from The Chicago School of Professional Psychology. My areas of expertise are in Behavior Modification, Depression, Anxiety, Trauma, PTSD and Grief. I use a Cognitive Behavioral and Humanistic approach to help my clients bring out the best in themselves. Whether you're feeling stuck, overwhelmed, or just in need of someone to walk alongside you, I’m here to help you become the best version of yourself.
